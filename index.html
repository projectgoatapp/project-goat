What is Project GOAT?

Project GOAT is a fast fictional football career generator. You scout a 16-year-old player, lock in their identity, then simulate their full career season by season.

The fun is in the story: first-team debuts, transfer madness, international call-ups, breakout seasons, trophies, awards, injuries, late-career moves and the final legacy judgement.

How to play

1
Choose your savePick Easy, Standard, Hard or Legendary. Easy lets you steer position and club pool. Hard asks if a defender or keeper can become the GOAT.
2
Scout a prospectName and nation lock in. Use the little reroll buttons on club, overall or potential until you run out of scout favours. Keep the lad or bin him off and scout someone else.
3
Start the careerWatch the timeline unfold: debut, season stats, transfers, trophies, awards, international moments and occasional chaos.
4
Chase the GOATRetire, see the club history, awards, trophies, earnings, average rating and GOAT score. Then immediately roll another one. It is the law.

Rules and modes

EasyPick position and starting club pool. Higher potential, kinder injuries, more rerolls and a more achievable GOAT run.
StandardRandom everything. Balanced chaos. The default career story generator.
HardDefensive positions only: GK, CB or DM. Fewer rerolls, no easy route, and top-tier legacy needs Ballon D’Goat proof.
LegendaryNo rerolls, no field fixing, harsher injuries and a brutal GOAT chase. The football gods are not your mate.

100/100 GOAT rule: to truly max the score, you need a record-breaking career: 8+ Ballon D’Goats, 4+ European Champions Cups and at least 1 World Championship.

Why Ballon D’Goat? Pencil pushers. Same golden glory, stupider name, fewer legal goblins.
